# Collaboration — Luca Caristina

My work is not task-based.
It is system-based.

---

## What I Look For

I collaborate with:

* companies working on AI integration and advanced digital systems
* teams developing complex platforms and multi-layer infrastructures
* startups exploring new technological paradigms
* professionals and SMEs building structured digital projects
* research-oriented environments and interdisciplinary initiatives

Projects where my work creates the most value are those where:

* complexity is high
* structure is not yet clearly defined
* multiple layers need to be integrated
* AI needs to become operational, not just experimental
* ideas exist, but lack a coherent system

My role is to bring:

* clarity where there is confusion

I typically work on:

## How I Work

I do not start from tools.

I start from:

* understanding the real problem
* defining the structure
* designing the system
* and only then moving to implementation

This ensures that:

* technology is used with purpose
* systems are coherent
* solutions are scalable

---

## What I Do Not Do

To maintain clarity and quality of work, I do not focus on:

* isolated technical tasks without context
* purely execution-based work without system design
* projects driven only by short-term output
* generic or undefined collaborations

## Ideal Starting Point

Most collaborations start with:

* an initial conversation
* a project overview
* identification of key challenges
* definition of the system scope

From there, we determine:
